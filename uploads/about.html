Our Story

Founded in 2024, Eventora was born from a simple idea: planning events should be exciting, not stressful. Our founders experienced firsthand the difficulty of coordinating multiple vendors, comparing prices, and ensuring quality. That's why we built a platform that brings everything together.

Today, Eventora is Sri Lanka's fastest-growing event planning platform, connecting thousands of customers with the best vendors in the industry. We're proud to have helped plan over 1,000 events, from intimate birthdays to grand weddings and corporate galas.
