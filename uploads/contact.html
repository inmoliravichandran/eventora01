Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a service?

Simply browse our services, select your preferred package, and click "Add to Cart". Then proceed to checkout to complete your booking. It takes less than 5 minutes!

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 7 days before the event date by contacting our support team or through your account dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

Is there a cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 14 days before the event. Cancellations within 14 days may incur a fee depending on the service.

Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely! Contact us with your requirements and we'll create a customized package tailored to your needs and budget.

How do I become a vendor?

We're always looking for quality vendors. Please email partnerships@eventora.com with your portfolio and services.
